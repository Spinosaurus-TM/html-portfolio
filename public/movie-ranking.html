The Best Movies According to Daood

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Dark Knight

This is my favorite movie. It has an intricate plot, aptly captures the dark Gotham vibe, something that I have a strong liking for. Explores deeper philosophical concepts of Justice, Anarchy, Crime and Integrity

The Dark Knight Rises

Love the character depiction. Wonderful ending

Coco

It touched a deeply emotional side of me as it beautifully touched upon the rather heavy concepts of life and death
